📘 Trading Instructions

Read before using indicators in live market.

⚠️ Indicators help decision making — they DO NOT predict market perfectly.
🔥 Practice is the key to consistency in trading.
✅

Professional Trading

  • Trade with market trend
  • Wait for confirmation candle
  • Use EMA + Structure + FVG
  • Use proper stop loss
  • Follow risk management
  • Trade patiently
❌

Beginner Mistakes

  • Entering randomly
  • Overtrading
  • Trading during news spikes
  • No stop loss
  • Ignoring market structure
  • Depending only on indicators

📊 Entry Confirmation Flow
